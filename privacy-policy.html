Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 4, 2024

Welcome to Bellmade! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our fitness training app. By accessing or using Bellmade, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you register for an account, purchase training programs, or subscribe to our app, we may collect personal information, including your name, email address, payment information, and profile information (such as profile picture and bio).

Usage Information

We collect information about how you use Bellmade, including the training programs you access, your progress and activity data, device information (such as device type and operating system), and log information (such as access times and pages viewed).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide and improve our services, process your transactions and manage your subscriptions, personalize your experience, communicate with you including updates and promotional materials, monitor and analyze usage to improve our app, and protect the security and integrity of our platform.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties except with your consent, to third-party service providers who assist us in operating our app (who agree to keep this information confidential), when required by law, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

4. Security of Your Information

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information, including encryption, access controls, and secure server environments. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices and Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information: Access (request access to your personal information), Correction (request to correct inaccurate information), Deletion (request deletion of your information, subject to legal obligations), Objection (object to processing in certain circumstances), and Data Portability (request a copy in a machine-readable format).

To delete your account and personal data, open the Bellmade app and go to Settings → Account → Delete Account.

For other requests, please contact us at admin@bellmade.app.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at admin@bellmade.app.
